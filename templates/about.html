What is The Lost Archive?


Our Star Wars fan film follows Dane, a teenaged orphan living with his brother on the planet Baeliv.
Dane has a vision, after which he abruply leaves home.
Where will his voyage lead him, and what will he find?
We hope you find out.

Where is The Lost Archive?

TLA is being filmed in the Hudson Valley, New York.
We're based in New Paltz, but are filming in areas all around.

When is The Lost Archive?

We're currently in the pre-production phase, but expect to start filming in May/June 2022.
After we do start filming, we should be done by Fall. We're hoping to be all done by the end of the year.
