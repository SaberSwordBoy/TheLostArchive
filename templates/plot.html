Plot Summary


Dane wakes up after having a vision, and sets off on a quest to find Grand Master Yoda's lightsaber.
He leaves his home planet and brother behind, stealing a ship and heading to Dagobah.
Meanwhile, someone else is buying a ship
Dane fights his way onto Dagobah and finds the lightsaber - and finds a friend.
After staying on Dagobah to train, when he tries to leave he encounters the Sith who killed his father.

What happens next? I guess you'll have to wait and see...
